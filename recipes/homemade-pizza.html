Homemade Pizza

Description

This pepperoni pizza recipe produces a quick and easy classic

Delicious homemade pizza crust and tomato sauce have never been easier

Ingredients

Pizza Sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 can contadina tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano, crushed
  • 1 tsp dried basil, crushed
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
Pizza Crust
  • 3 1/4 cup flour
  • 2 envelopes fleischmanns pizza crust yeast
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 1/3 cup very warm water
  • 1/3 cup oil
Toppings
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 package of pepperoni

Steps

  1. Whisk together ingredients for sauce in a medium bowl until smooth
  2. Mix ingredients for crust until well blended, then add remaining flour until a soft, sticky dough forms
  3. Transfer dough to a floured surface and knead until smooth and elastic
  4. Divide dough in half and place onto pizza pans
  5. Top dough with sauce, cheese and pepperoni
  6. Bake until crust is browned and cheese is bubbly; Around 18-20 minutes
