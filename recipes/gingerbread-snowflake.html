Gingerbread Snowflakes

Description

This recipe for gingerbread snowflakes uses my favorite gingersnap cookie recipe

What a great starting point for these delicious six-pointed cookies

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 tsp ground ginger
  • 1 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp fine salt
  • 1/4 tsp ground cloves
  • 12 tbsp unsalted butter, softened
  • 2/3 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 cup molasses
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 large egg

Steps

  1. Whisk 2 cups flour, 2 tsp, cocoa powder, ginger, cinnamon, baking soda, salt and cloves
  2. Cream together butter and sugar in a separate bowl for 2 mins
  3. Add molasses, vanilla and egg to butter and sugar mixture and whisk
  4. Set dough on plastic wrap and shape into a rectangle, then refrigreate until cold and firm
  5. Prehead oven to 350 degrees farenheit
  6. After the dough has chilled cut into desired snowflake shape
  7. Bake for about 12 mins for chewy and 15 for more crispy
